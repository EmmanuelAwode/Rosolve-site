Who We Are

At Rosolve, our mission is to revolutionize transportation with inclusive, tech-powered solutions. Our core values include safety, empowerment, and community.

Our flagship project, SheGo, is a ride-hailing service built by women, for women—designed to ensure safer, more confident travel experiences. We are a growing team of developers, designers, and entrepreneurs with a passion for impact.

What We Do

Urban Mobility Solutions

We design transport solutions for underserved demographics, ensuring inclusive and safe mobility experiences.

Tech Consulting

Rosolve provides consulting on app development, system design, and implementation for startups and civic innovation.

Startup Product Development

From idea to launch, we help entrepreneurs build and scale tech products that address real-world problems.

Projects

SheGo

SheGo is our flagship ride-hailing platform for women. It connects female passengers with female drivers, providing safer transport and economic opportunities.
